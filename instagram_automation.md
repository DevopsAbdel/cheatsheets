## 3. Copy & Paste This AI Prompt

Use this master prompt in Claude AI to plan your entire month of content in seconds.

![Copy This Prompt](1000075881.jpg)

### The Master Prompt Blueprint
```text
You are a world-class Instagram content strategist.
Your task is to help me create 30 days of content for my niche.

Generate 30 viral Instagram content ideas.
For each idea, provide the following:

1. Hook (attention-grabbing opening line)
2. Reel Script (short, engaging, ready-to-record script)
3. Carousel Outline (slide-by-slide key points)
4. Caption (engaging caption with value)
5. CTA (clear call-to-action)
6. Target Audience (who is this for)
7. Engagement Trigger (what will make people like, comment, share or save)

IMPORTANT RULES:
• Make content highly viral and engaging.
• Use simple language that connects with my audience.
• Make hooks super strong and curiosity-driven.
• Keep scripts short, punchy and easy to say.
• Provide practical, actionable and shareable content.

Return the output in a clean, structured format.
Make it easy for me to copy and use.
```

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Customize the prompt by adding your specific **Niche**, **Tone**, **Target Audience**, and **Goal** to get even better, hyper-targeted results.
