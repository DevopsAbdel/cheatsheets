## 1. What is Vibe-Coding?
Vibe-coding is a modern approach to web development that prioritizes rapid prototyping, AI-assisted design, and modular building. By leveraging agentic AI and prompt libraries, you can "describe" what you want your app to do, and the tools handle the technical implementation.

## 2. Top 5 Tools for Your Workflow
Here are the essential websites to streamline your process:

- **[VibeUI.online](https://vibeui.online)**: A massive repository of ready-made prompts for UI components (Hero sections, Authentication forms, Pricing tables, Dashboards). Perfect for copy-pasting into AI code generators.
- **[MotionSites.ai](https://motionsites.ai)**: Focuses on stunning, high-end animated website templates. You can access the specific prompts needed to recreate these advanced designs with one click.
- **[Manus.im](https://manus.im)**: An agentic AI builder. This tool doesn't just write code; it acts as an agent to build entire websites or mobile apps based on your natural language descriptions. Requires zero coding knowledge.
- **[Trickle.so](https://trickle.so/blog/trickle-vibe-coding-prompt-library)**: A library of 50+ curated prompts designed to unblock your creativity and speed up your workflow for specific design and functionality challenges.
- **[Blog.VibeCoder.me](https://blog.vibecoder.me)**: A resource for fill-in-the-blank prompt templates. It covers everything from basic UI layouts to complex API integrations, database setups, and automated testing.

## 3. How to Get Started
1. **Define Your Goal**: Clearly write down what your website should do (e.g., "A landing page for a coffee shop with a booking button").
2. **Browse Components**: Use **VibeUI** to find the layouts for your sections.
3. **Draft with AI**: Take those layouts to **Manus.im** and describe your vision, plugging in the prompts you found.
4. **Refine**: If you get stuck, consult the **Trickle** or **VibeCoder blog** to find prompts that solve specific technical hurdles.

## 4. Tips for Success
- **Be Specific**: The better you describe your vision (e.g., "Use a brutalist design," "Include a dark mode toggle"), the better the output.
- **Iterate**: AI isn't always perfect on the first try. Don't be afraid to ask the AI to "refine the spacing" or "change the color palette to be more professional."
- **Focus on the Vibe**: Your goal is to get a working product that *feels* right. Let the tools handle the syntax so you can focus on the design and functionality.
